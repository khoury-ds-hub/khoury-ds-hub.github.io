About the Data Sessions

2020-01-23 22:00:00

Our Data Science Sessions are an opportunity for students interested in Data Science to gather and work on applied projects together.

It is through these sessions that we hope to bring students together to build and devise interesting solutions to machine learning challenges. Think of it as a sort of Hackathon for Data Science. A Datathon, maybe?

In addition, starting with our February 5th session, we are now introducing a lecture series to be held during the first half of all data sessions where we invite graduate students to discuss different topics in the Data Science field.
